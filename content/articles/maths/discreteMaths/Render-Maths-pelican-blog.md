## De Morgan's Law ##

* De Morgan’s Law (negation of conjunction)

$$ \neg(P \wedge Q) \equiv \neg P \vee \neg Q $$

* De Morgan’s Law (negation of alternative)

$$ \neg(P \vee Q) \equiv \neg P \wedge \neg Q $$
